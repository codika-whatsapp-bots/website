## Who We Are

WhatsApp Bots is a service provided by **Codika SRL**, a company registered in Belgium. We build AI-powered WhatsApp bots paired with admin dashboards for community and business management.

**Contact:** hello@codika.io

## What Data We Collect

### When You Visit Our Website

- **Usage data:** pages visited, time spent, referral source, browser type, device type, and IP address
- **Cookies:** we use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site

### When You Use Our Service (as a Client)

- **Account information:** name, email address, organization name
- **WhatsApp data:** phone numbers, message content, and conversation history processed through our bot workflows
- **Dashboard data:** member records, event data, organization data, and any other information you enter into your admin dashboard

### When You Interact With a Bot (as an End User)

- **WhatsApp messages:** the content of messages you send to or receive from a WhatsApp bot powered by our platform
- **Phone number:** your WhatsApp phone number, used to identify you and maintain conversation context
- **Profile data:** any information you voluntarily share during conversations (name, preferences, etc.)

## How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

- **Provide our service:** process WhatsApp messages, run AI-powered bot workflows, and display data in admin dashboards
- **Maintain conversation context:** store conversation history so the bot can provide relevant, personalized responses
- **Improve our service:** analyze usage patterns to improve bot performance and user experience
- **Communicate with you:** send service-related notifications and respond to support requests
- **Comply with legal obligations:** meet regulatory requirements applicable to our business

## How AI Processes Your Data

Our bots use AI language models (currently Claude by Anthropic) to understand and respond to messages. When you interact with a bot:

- Your message is sent to the AI model for processing
- The AI model generates a response based on the conversation context and available tools
- We do not use your messages to train AI models
- AI-generated responses may occasionally be inaccurate — they should not be treated as professional advice

## Data Storage and Security

- **Infrastructure:** our platform runs on Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and n8n workflow automation, with dashboards powered by Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- **WhatsApp messages:** processed through the official Twilio WhatsApp Business API (Meta-approved)
- **Encryption:** data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
- **Access control:** only authorized team members can access client data, and only for service delivery and support purposes
- **Data location:** data is primarily stored in EU and US data centers

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

- **Service providers:** Twilio (WhatsApp messaging), Anthropic (AI processing), Google Cloud (infrastructure), Supabase (database), and other providers necessary for service delivery
- **Legal requirements:** when required by law, court order, or regulatory authority
- **Business transfers:** in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

All service providers are bound by data processing agreements and are required to protect your data.

## Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Export** your data in a portable format
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw consent** at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **hello@codika.io**. We will respond within 30 days.

## Data Retention

- **Client data:** retained for the duration of the service agreement, plus 90 days after termination
- **Conversation history:** retained as long as the client's bot is active, unless the client requests earlier deletion
- **Website analytics:** aggregated data retained indefinitely; individual data deleted after 26 months
- **Account data:** deleted within 90 days of account closure

## Baileys Connector (Self-Hosted)

Some clients use our open-source Baileys connector template to enable WhatsApp group support. When using the Baileys connector:

- The connector runs on the **client's own infrastructure** — we do not host or operate it
- Messages flow from the client's server to our webhook endpoints for processing
- We process these messages the same way as messages received through the official Twilio channel
- The client is responsible for compliance with Meta's Terms of Service regarding the use of unofficial WhatsApp protocols

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.
